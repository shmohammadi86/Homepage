Me, Myself & Shahin

I am a computer scientist, but also have extensive background and publication record in the broad areas of Bioinformatics and Computational Biology, as well as extra-curricular training in molecular biology and pharmacology. The wealth of accumulated data, the emerging systems-level understanding, and the ability to precisely profile biological processes, poses fundamental challenges that require a mix of analytical, algorithmic, and modeling techniques. My main expertize is in designing computational methods coupled with statistical models to analyze the ever-increasing body of Big Data generated from high-throughput technologies. For a detailed summary of my work, please visit my Research Summary page.
